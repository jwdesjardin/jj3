About Me

I made the decision to completey throw myself into web development in the spring of 2020. I had been learning to program for several years and felt there was no better time to reach for my dream of engineering for a living. I have fully immersed myself in the best quality programming content I could find from brands like Treehouse, Udemy, Educative and Youtube for several months to get comfortable in the ecosystem.
